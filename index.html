For my third year 3D anmiation class, one of the more demanding assignments was to create a 30-40 second 3D animation.

This involved finding models to use, creating the scene, animating it, setting up cameras, rendering, editing, and performing any other post production.

Arduino Sythesizer

I had an interesting course in third called Sensor-Based Interaction, an advanced programming class focusing on hardware, sensors, and interactive installations. The final project was to create something strange and interactive in a team of three using three randomly chosen sensors.

We chose a synthesizer. A force sensor controlled the volume, a joystick controlled which chord was being played, and a distance sensor controlled the number of voices.

Mobile Phone Public Service Announcement

The final project for my second year AV course was to make a sixty second video in the style of a comercial, or a public service announcement.

This was done in teams of four, but the groupwork only covered the scripting, filming and production. All editing and post-production was done by each team member - meaning we each had our own version.

This is my version, which was produced using Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects.

Population Infographic

One of the most enjoyable video projects I've worked on, this assignment involved creating an infographic about a topic of our choice, using Adobe After Effects.

It was particularly interesting to me because it was more or less my first time creating something just using After Effects, and though I remember feeling pleased with the result, I was sure I could do significantly better the next time, just because of how much I'd learned.
